## Detailed Information

Agricultural Harvesting automates complex agricultural field management. Mobile robotic platforms equipped with GPI stacks traverse irregular terrains, evaluating crop ripeness indices via multimodal visual filters, and executing precision harvesting actions on delicate fruits without bruising the organic membranes.
